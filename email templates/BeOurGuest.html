WHAT IS OUR UNIVERSE LIKE?

Stellar Sound Podcast is a podcast in English with one guest from the European music industry per episode.

The episodes consist of discussions between the interviewer and the guest over cherry-picked topics matching the guest’s personality, style, and wishes.

The episodes are created during the video call; the podcast is created in an audio format and hosted on the most common podcast platforms like Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, many other podcast platforms, and YouTube too.
